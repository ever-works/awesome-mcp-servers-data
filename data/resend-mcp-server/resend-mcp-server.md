## Overview

The Resend MCP server is an MCP server for the Resend platform that allows you to send and receive emails, manage contacts, broadcasts, domains, and more — directly from any MCP client like Claude Desktop, Cursor, or Claude Code.

Resend has quickly become a favorite among developers for its clean API, excellent documentation, and focus on deliverability. Resend is a modern, developer-first email platform focused on a stellar developer experience and ensuring your emails actually reach human inboxes, not spam folders.

## Key Features

### Email Operations
- Send, list, get, cancel, update, and batch send emails
- Support for HTML, plain text, attachments (local file, URL, or base64)
- CC/BCC, reply-to, scheduling, tags, and topic-based sending

### Inbound Email
- List and read inbound emails
- List and download received email attachments

### Contact Management
- Create, list, get, update, and remove contacts
- Manage segment memberships and topic subscriptions
- Support for custom contact properties

### Broadcast Campaigns
- Create, send, list, get, update, and remove broadcast campaigns
- Support for scheduling, personalization placeholders, and preview text

## Use Cases

You can let Cursor or Claude Desktop compose emails for you and send it right away without having to copy and paste the email content. Examples include:
- Run local scripts, chat with Claude, or process data and send the results to yourself or your team
- Automated email workflows
- Customer communication automation
- Newsletter and campaign management
- Transactional email sending
